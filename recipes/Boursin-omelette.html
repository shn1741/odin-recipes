Boursin Omelette

Description

Boursin Omelette is a simple, delicious omelette that is perfect for breakfast or brunch. It is made with eggs, Boursin cheese, and fresh herbs, and is flavored with salt and pepper. This omelette is light and fluffy, with a creamy, cheesy filling that is sure to satisfy your taste buds. It is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite ingredients, such as vegetables, meats, or other cheeses.
