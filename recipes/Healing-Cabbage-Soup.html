Healing Cabbage Soup

Description

Healing Cabbage Soup is a simple, healthy soup that is perfect for a light meal. It is made with cabbage, carrots, onions, and celery, and is flavored with garlic, ginger, and turmeric. This soup is low in calories and high in fiber, making it a great choice for anyone looking to lose weight or improve their health. It is also vegan and gluten-free, so it is suitable for a variety of dietary restrictions.
